The Process

A structured way through an unstructured decision

Career change rarely fails for lack of ambition. It fails for lack of a plan. Here's the order we work in.

01

Diagnose the real problem

We separate "I hate my job" from "I'm in the wrong industry" — they need very different solutions.

02

Build the bridge

We map which of your existing skills transfer, what's missing, and the fastest credible path to close the gap.

03

Make the move

Positioning, applications, and interview narrative — built around the story of why you're a strong bet, not an outsider.
