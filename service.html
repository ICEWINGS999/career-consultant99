Services

Three ways to work together, depending on how far you've gotten.

Most clients start with a Clarity Session, even if they end up needing more — it's the cheapest way to find out if a bigger commitment is worth making.

01 — Single Session

Clarity Session

For people who feel stuck but can't yet name why. A 90-minute working session, not a chat — you'll leave with a clear read on what's actually wrong and a concrete next step.

Includes

Problem diagnosis

We separate dissatisfaction with the job from dissatisfaction with the field.

Includes

Direction shortlist

2–3 realistic directions based on your actual transferable skills.

Includes

Written summary

A short follow-up note with what we covered and your next step.

02 — Most Booked

Transition Roadmap

A 6-week engagement for people who know they want to move, but not exactly where or how. We build the actual plan — not just motivation.

Week 1–2

Skills audit

A full inventory of what transfers, what's missing, and how to talk about both.

Week 3–4

Target research

We test 2–3 target paths against the real job market, not just interest.

Week 5–6

The roadmap

A written 6–12 month plan: positioning, credentials if needed, and outreach strategy.

03 — Full Support

Application & Interview Partner

For people actively applying. I work alongside you through the messy middle — reframing your story, prepping for interviews, and troubleshooting in real time.

Ongoing

Resume & story reframing

Presenting your background so a new industry can actually recognize it.

Ongoing

Mock interviews

Practice for the "why are you switching" question until it's your strongest answer, not your weakest.

Ongoing

On-call troubleshooting

Async support when an offer, rejection, or curveball question needs a fast read.
